Hannah Barlag

Hannah Barlag is a mural artist based in Berlin. Co-founder of Barlag Wandmalerei® art studio in 2016. Her professional practice encompasses fresco & mural, traditional fine art, and digital sculpting.

Works in private collections in the Netherlands, Belgium, Switzerland, Chile, the Baltic States, Denmark, Russia, United Arab Emirates, Germany, Italy, the United Kingdom, and the United States of America.
